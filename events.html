Ongoing Opportunities

The Fleet Science Center is offering after school and Saturday Teacher Professional Development free of charge! You can also join their Teacher Partner Program for free admission and access to other great opportunities.

Some great sessions coming up include Sustainable Strategies for Business, LEED Green Associate Training, Build a Better Burger with a focus on commercial kitchens, and more. You can also bring your students on a tour! Let us know if you're interested and we can help get you set up.
